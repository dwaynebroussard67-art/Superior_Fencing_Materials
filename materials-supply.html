Everything You Need to Build a Fence — In Stock and Ready

Superior Building Materials is a full-service fencing supply yard serving homeowners, contractors, and businesses in South Louisiana. We stock the same commercial-grade materials we use in our own installations — posts, panels, wire, hardware, concrete, and more.

Whether you're a DIY homeowner building your own fence or a contractor ordering materials for a job site, you'll get honest pricing, expert advice, and the materials you need when you need them. Pickup or delivery available.

What We Stock

  • Wood fence posts (treated 4x4, 6x6, and larger)
  • Cedar and pressure-treated pine pickets and panels
  • Vinyl fence posts, panels, and pickets
  • Chain-link fabric (galvanized and vinyl-coated)
  • Chain-link posts, rails, and fittings
  • Ornamental aluminum and steel panels
  • Welded wire mesh and field fence
  • Barbed wire and razor wire
  • Steel and aluminum gate frames
  • Gate hinges, latches, and hardware
  • Post caps, brackets, and mounting hardware
  • Concrete mix and ready-mix options
  • Wire ties, tensioning tools, and accessories
  • Privacy slats and windscreen
  • Post hole diggers and hand tools

Who We Serve

DIY Homeowners

Building your own fence? We'll help you figure out what you need, how much to buy, and how to do it right. No upselling. Just honest advice from people who build fences every day. You'll get the same quality materials we use in our own installations.

Contractors & Builders

We're a contractor's supply house. Open an account, get volume pricing, and never wait for materials. We keep high-demand items in stock and can deliver to your job site. Call ahead and we'll have your order ready for pickup.

Property Managers & Maintenance Crews

Need replacement posts, panels, or hardware for an existing fence? We stock repair materials and can match most common fence styles. Fast turnaround for urgent repairs.

Ranchers & Landowners

Field fence, barbed wire, heavy-duty posts, and livestock-safe materials. We supply what works in South Louisiana — materials that stand up to the weather and the workload.

Why Buy From Us

Commercial-Grade Quality

Honest Pricing

Expert Advice

Talk to people who build fences, not retail clerks. We'll help you calculate quantities, choose the right materials, and avoid costly mistakes.

Pickup or Delivery

Load it yourself or we'll deliver to your job site. Delivery available throughout South Louisiana.
