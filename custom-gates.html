Gates Built the Right Way — By Us, For You

This is what sets us apart. While most fence companies outsource custom work or don't offer it at all, we forge, weld, and finish every custom gate ourselves, right here in Broussard.

When you order a custom gate from Superior Building Materials, you're getting a piece of functional art. Our ironworkers hand-build each gate to your exact specifications, using commercial-grade steel and time-tested welding techniques. No two gates are identical. Every one is a statement.

From ornamental driveway gates to heavy-duty ranch gates to decorative garden entries, we build gates that last generations in Louisiana's climate.

The Custom Gate Process

1. Consultation & Design

We start by understanding your property, your style, and how you'll use the gate. Swing or slide? Automated or manual? Ornamental or functional? We'll walk the site, take measurements, and work with you to design a gate that fits perfectly.

2. Fabrication In-House

Once the design is approved, our ironworkers get to work. Every gate is welded by hand in our Broussard shop. We use commercial-grade steel, precision cuts, and time-tested metalworking techniques. You're welcome to visit and see your gate taking shape.

3. Finishing & Coating

Every gate is ground smooth, primed, and finished with industrial-grade powder coating or paint designed to withstand Louisiana sun, rain, and humidity. We don't rush this step — the finish is what protects your investment for decades.

4. Professional Installation

Our licensed crew installs the gate with the same care we put into building it. Proper alignment, strong posts, smooth operation — we don't leave until it swings or slides perfectly.

Why Our Custom Gates Are Different

Built to Last Generations

We don't use thin-gauge hobby steel or pre-fab kits. Every gate is built with commercial-grade materials and engineered to handle Louisiana weather for 30+ years.

You Work Directly With the Builders

No sales reps. No middlemen. You'll talk to the ironworkers who are actually building your gate. That means your vision doesn't get lost in translation.

Fast Turnaround for Custom Work

Because we control the entire process in-house, we can complete most custom gates in 3–6 weeks from design approval to installation. Rush jobs? Call us — we'll make it work.
