Why Superior Building Materials Is Your Best Choice

We're not the biggest company in Louisiana. We're the fastest growing — and there's a reason. We outwork, outbuild, and out-deliver the competition every single day.

In-House Custom Gate Fabrication

Most fence companies outsource their custom work or don't offer it at all. We forge, weld, and finish every custom gate ourselves, right here in Broussard. That means you work directly with the people building your gate. No middleman. No excuses. Just honest craftsmanship and direct accountability.

When you order a custom driveway gate, entry gate, or ornamental piece from us, you're getting a one-of-a-kind product made by Louisiana ironworkers who take pride in every weld.

Speed & Reliability You Can Count On

We're the fastest growing fencing and gate company in South Louisiana because we deliver — on time, every time. Our turnaround times beat the competition because we control every step of the process, from material sourcing to final installation.

Contractors trust us to keep their job sites moving. Homeowners trust us to show up when we say we will. That reliability has built our reputation, and it's why our customer base grows every month.

Materials Quality & Selection Under One Roof

We're both a supply house and a fabrication shop. That means we source commercial-grade materials in bulk, pass the savings to you, and install the exact same products we sell. No bait-and-switch. No surprise upgrades.

Whether you're a DIY homeowner picking up materials or a contractor ordering a full fence package, you get the same honest pricing and the same quality we put into our own installations.

Local, Family-Owned, Accountable

Superior Building Materials is owned and operated by the Hebert family. We live in South Louisiana. Our kids go to school here. Our reputation is our livelihood. When you call, you talk to us — not a call center in another state.

Being local means we understand Louisiana weather, soil conditions, and building practices. It also means if something goes wrong, you know exactly where to find us. That accountability matters.

Workmanship & Warranty We Stand Behind

We don't just build fences and gates. We build relationships. Every installation is backed by our workmanship guarantee, and we use materials designed to last decades in South Louisiana's climate.

If there's ever an issue, we make it right. No runaround. No fine print. Just a handshake and a promise that we'll stand behind what we build.
