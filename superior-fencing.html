Superior Fencing — Installation You Can Trust

Superior Building Materials provides the materials. Superior Fencing handles the installation. Same family. Same commitment to quality. One phone call gets you both.

Whether you're a homeowner who wants a turnkey fence installation, a contractor who needs reliable subcontractors, or a business securing a commercial property, our licensed installation crew delivers professional results on time and on budget.

We install everything we sell — and we stand behind every job.

One Call Does It All

You don't need to figure out whether to call the supply house or the installation company. Call 337-400-7838 and we'll route you to the right side. If you need materials only, we'll handle that. If you need full installation, we'll handle that. If you're not sure, we'll walk you through your options.

For Homeowners — Turnkey Installation

You want a new fence or gate, but you don't want to manage the project yourself. We get it. Call us, and we'll schedule a site visit, measure your property, recommend the right materials, provide a clear written quote, and handle the entire installation from start to finish. You approve the plan, we do the work, and when we're done, you have a fence or gate that will last decades.

What's included: Site consultation, materials, permitting assistance (if needed), professional installation, cleanup, and a workmanship guarantee.

For DIY Homeowners — Materials & Advice

You're handy and want to build it yourself. We respect that. Come to our supply yard, talk to our team, and we'll help you calculate what you need, choose the right materials, and avoid common mistakes. You'll get the same commercial-grade materials we use in our own installations, plus honest advice from people who build fences every day.

We'll help you with: Material selection, quantity calculations, tool recommendations, installation tips, and troubleshooting if you hit a snag.

For Contractors — Reliable Supply & Installation Partnership

You're a contractor managing a project, and you need a local fencing partner you can count on. We work with contractors every day. Open an account, get contractor pricing on materials, and if you need installation support, our crew can handle the fencing scope of your job while you manage the rest. We show up when we say we will, we work clean, and we don't hold up your schedule.

Contractor benefits: Volume pricing, dedicated account support, job-site delivery, flexible scheduling, and licensed, insured installation crews.

For Commercial & Industrial Clients — Spec Work & Code Compliance

You need security fencing, access gates, or perimeter fencing for a commercial or industrial property. You need it done to code, on schedule, and with minimal disruption to your operations. Our installation team has handled projects for warehouses, contractors, property managers, and businesses across South Louisiana. We understand commercial requirements, permitting, and deadlines.

Commercial capabilities: Large-scale chain-link, ornamental steel, automatic gates, access control integration, ADA-compliant installations, and post-project support.

For Custom Gate Projects — Fabrication & Installation

You want a one-of-a-kind custom gate — ornamental iron, a grand driveway entrance, a ranch gate with your brand, or a commercial security gate built to exact specifications. We forge it in-house, and our crew installs it with the same precision we put into building it. From design consultation to final walk-through, you work directly with the people welding and installing your gate.

Custom gate process: Design consultation, in-house fabrication, powder coating or custom finish, professional installation, automation (if desired), and final adjustment to ensure smooth operation.
