Family-Run. Locally Trusted. Built to Last.

Superior Building Materials is a family-owned fencing supply and custom gate fabrication company serving South Louisiana. We do two things exceptionally well: we supply the materials contractors and homeowners need, and we build custom gates in-house that are forged to last generations.

Whether you're picking up materials for your own project, need a complete fence installed, or want a one-of-a-kind driveway gate, you'll work directly with people who know fencing inside and out. We're not a call center. We're your neighbors.

Owned and operated by the Hebert family in Broussard, Louisiana, we've earned our reputation by showing up, delivering on time, and standing behind every piece of work we do.
