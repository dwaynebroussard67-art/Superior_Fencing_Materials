Automatic Gate Systems

Convenience, security, and access control for any gate.

Call 337-400-7838

Automate Your Gate — Add Security, Save Time

An automatic gate opener transforms how you use your property. No more getting out of your truck in the rain to open your driveway gate. No more worrying about whether you remembered to close the gate. Just press a button and go.

We install, service, and repair automatic gate systems for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Whether you're automating a new custom gate or upgrading an existing manual gate, we'll recommend the right opener, configure the controls, and make sure it operates smoothly for years.

What We Offer

Swing Gate Openers

Single or dual swing configurations. Hydraulic or electromechanical operators for gates up to 1,000+ lbs.

Slide Gate Openers

Chain-drive, belt-drive, or rack-and-pinion systems for sliding gates. Residential and commercial-duty.

Keypads & Access Control

Wireless keypads, card readers, smartphone control, and intercom systems for secure property access.

Remotes & Transmitters

Multi-channel remotes, vehicle transmitters, and smartphone apps — control your gate from anywhere.

Solar Power Options

Off-grid solar kits for remote gates. Battery backup for power outages. Energy-efficient and reliable.

Maintenance & Repair

Existing system not working? We service all major brands, replace worn parts, and get your gate running again.

Why Choose Us for Gate Automation

We Build Gates, So We Know How to Automate Them

Because we fabricate and install gates ourselves, we understand the mechanics, weight distribution, and structural requirements that make automation reliable. We match the right opener to your gate's size, weight, and duty cycle.

Quality Components That Last

We install professional-grade gate operators from trusted manufacturers — LiftMaster, DoorKing, Viking Access, and others. These aren't residential garage door openers retrofitted for gates. They're purpose-built for outdoor, heavy-duty use.

Full Service — Installation, Wiring, Programming

We handle the entire installation: running power, mounting the operator, wiring safety sensors, programming remotes and keypads, and testing. When we're done, your gate opens and closes smoothly every single time.

Maintenance & Emergency Repair

Gates break. Openers wear out. We service what we install — and fix what others installed. Call us and we'll troubleshoot, repair, or replace components to get your gate operational again.
