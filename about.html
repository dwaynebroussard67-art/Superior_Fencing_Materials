Our Story

Who We Are

Superior Building Materials is a family-owned fencing supply and custom gate fabrication company based in Broussard, Louisiana. We're owned and operated by Braylen Hebert, and we've built our reputation one fence and one gate at a time — by showing up, doing honest work, and standing behind every project.

How We Got Here

We started with a simple idea: South Louisiana needed a fencing company that could do two things exceptionally well — supply quality materials at fair prices, and build custom gates that weren't available anywhere else.

Most fence companies are either supply houses that don't install, or installation crews that don't fabricate custom work. We do both. That integration gives our customers one point of contact, faster turnaround, and the confidence that the people selling the materials are the same people welding the gates and installing the fences.

Today, we're the fastest growing fencing and custom gate business in South Louisiana. That growth didn't come from marketing. It came from word of mouth — homeowners, contractors, and business owners telling their neighbors about the quality of our work and the integrity of our team.

Meet the Team

When you call Superior Building Materials, you'll talk to Braylen Hebert or one of our experienced team members. We're a small crew, and that's by design. Everyone who works here knows fencing inside and out, and everyone takes ownership of the customer experience.

Our fabrication team includes skilled ironworkers who've been welding for decades. Our installation crews are licensed, experienced, and professional. Our supply yard staff can answer technical questions and help you calculate exactly what you need for your project.

You won't get shuffled to a call center. You won't be just a number. You'll work with people who genuinely care about getting your fence or gate done right.
