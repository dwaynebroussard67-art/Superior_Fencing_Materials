Fencing That Fits Your Home & Your Life

Your fence does more than mark property lines. It keeps your kids and pets safe, gives you privacy, and adds curb appeal to your home. We build residential fences that do all three — and last for decades in South Louisiana's climate.

Whether you need a classic wood privacy fence, a decorative picket fence, or low-maintenance vinyl, we'll help you choose the right material and design for your property. Every fence we install is built to withstand Louisiana heat, humidity, and storms.

What We Offer

Wood Privacy Fences

Cedar and pressure-treated pine. Classic, affordable, and customizable. Perfect for backyards.

Picket Fences

Charming, traditional, and timeless. Wood or vinyl options for front yards and garden borders.

Vinyl Fencing

Low-maintenance, never needs painting, resists rot and insects. Clean modern look that lasts.

Ornamental Aluminum & Steel

Elegant, strong, and rust-resistant. Classic wrought-iron look without the maintenance.

Chain-Link Fencing

Affordable, durable, secure. Great for pet containment and backyard boundaries.

Fence Repair & Replacement

Storm damage? Rotted posts? We repair and replace sections or entire fences.

Why Choose Us for Your Home Fence

We Install What We Sell

Every material we install comes from our own supply yard. You get commercial-grade quality at honest residential pricing, and we stand behind every board and post.

Built for Louisiana Weather

We've been building fences in South Louisiana long enough to know what works. Proper drainage, treated lumber, concrete set correctly — the details matter, and we get them right.

Fast, Professional Installation

Our licensed crews show up on time, work clean, and finish on schedule. Most residential fences are completed in 1–3 days depending on size.
